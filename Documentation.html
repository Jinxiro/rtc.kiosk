PROGRAM OFFERINGS

3-Year Diploma Courses

Pursuant to Executive Order No. 83 Series of 2012 also known as the Philippine Qualifications Framework (PQF), the Center is now piloting 3-Year Diploma programs in Level 5 category (based on PQF 8-Level Descriptors) considering RTC-KorPhil Davao as the Center of Excellence for TVET in Mindanao.

Short-Term / Skills Upgrading Programs

Courses designed to accommodate workers, for life-long training, and for those who did not avail the 3-Year Diploma programs. Qualifications (courses with TESDA Training Regulations or TR) in diploma programs are also offered individually in separate schedules.

Community-Based Skills Training

In cooperation with Community Leaders (like political, church, or organization leaders) skills trainings are brought to the community to reach out the grassroots. Venues and schedules are depending on the stipulations in the Memorandum of Agreement (MOA) between the Center & the sponsoring partner.

Enrollment Guide

Where to GO?

Admin Building

Once you arrive at the Korphil Davao campus, your first step will be to locate the Admin Building. This is where the administrative offices are housed, and it's your key starting point for the enrollment process. If you're unfamiliar with the campus, feel free to ask for directions from any staff or students you encounter—they’ll be happy to guide you.

"Pag-abot nimo sa Korphil Davao campus, ang una nimo buhaton mao ang pagpangita sa Admin Building. Dinhi makita ang mga opisina sa administrasyon, ug kini ang imung unang lakang alang sa enrollment process. Kung wala kay klaro nga ideya sa lugar, pwede ka mangutana sa mga empleyado o estudyante nga imong makit-an para sa mga direksyon—sigurado nga andam sila mutabang nimo."

Left Turn

After entering the Admin Building, you will need to turn left to locate the Training Department. This department manages all aspects of training and student enrollments.

"Pag-sulod nimo sa Admin Building, liko sa wala aron makit-an nimo ang Training Department. Ang departmento nga kini ang nagdumala sa tanan nga bahin sa training ug student enrollments."

Hallway

walk down the hallway and look for the second door on your right—this is the entrance to the Training Department. For your convenience, the names of each room are clearly displayed on signs located above the doors. Look for the sign that says "Training Department" to ensure you're in the right place.

"Paglakaw sa Hallway ug pangitaa ang ikaduhang pultahan sa imong tuo—mao kini ang Training Department. Para sa imong kaginhawaan, ang mga ngalan sa matag kwarto makita sa ibabaw nga bahin sa pultahan. Pangitaa ang ngalan nga "Training Department" aron masiguro nga naa ka sa sakto nga dapit."

Training Department

After entering the Training Department, approach the staff and ask for assistance with the enrollment process. They will guide you through the necessary steps, including any paperwork and requirements you need to fulfill.

"Pag-sulod nimo sa Training Department, duola ang mga staff ug pangutana bahin sa enrollment process. I-guide ka nila sa mga kinahanglan buhaton, lakip na ang mga papeles ug mga requirements nga kinahanglan nimo kumplituhon."

Enrollment Process

Diploma Course

Blue Form

Please complete this form accurately and attach the required documents. For assistance, contact the admissions office. Thank you for choosing RTC KorPhil Davao!

This is the Front Page of the form where you must provide your personal details, including your name, address, contact information, and educational background. Please fill out all fields accurately and completely to ensure proper registration and processing.

Back Page includes details such as scholarship information, disability classification (if applicable), and a privacy disclaimer. Please review and complete all sections carefully. Your signature at the bottom certifies that the information provided is true and correct.

Manpower Profile

Please complete the Manpower Profile section with your full name (Last Name, First Name, Middle Name) and other required details. Ensure all information is accurate and up-to-date.
Note: The Unique Learner Identifier (ULI) Number is only for returning students. If you are a new student enrolling for the first time, leave this blank as the Training Department will provide your ULI.

Palihug kumpletoha ang Manpower Profile section uban ang imong bug-os nga ngalan (Apelyido, Pangalan, Middle Name) ug ubang gikinahanglan nga detalye. Siguruha nga tanan nga impormasyon tukma ug bag-o.
Pahibalo: Ang Unique Learner Identifier (ULI) Number para lamang sa mga balik-eskwela. Kung bag-o ka nga estudyante ug nagpa-enroll sa una nga higayon, ayaw kini pagsulati kay ang Training Department ang mohatag niini.

Personal Information

This section requires your personal details, including sex, civil status, employment status (before training), birthdate, birthplace, and educational attainment. Additionally, provide the name, address, and contact number of your emergency contact person. Ensure all information is accurate and complete, as it will be used for your training records and emergency purposes.

Kini nga seksyon nagkinahanglan sa imong personal nga detalye, lakip na ang sekso, estado sibil, estado sa trabaho (sa wala pa ang pagbansay), adlawng natawhan, lugar nga natawhan, ug natapos nga edukasyon. Dugang pa, ihatag ang ngalan, address, ug kontak numero sa imong emergency contact person. Siguruha nga tanan nga impormasyon tukma ug kompleto, kay kini gamiton alang sa imong training records ug emergency nga mga katuyuan.

LEARNER/TRAINEE/STUDENT CLASSIFICATION

This section identifies your classification as a learner/trainee/student. Please check the appropriate box that applies to you (e.g., 4Ps Beneficiary, Displaced Worker, Out-of-School Youth, etc.). If you have a disability, the TESDA personnel will assist in filling out the related details. Your response helps us tailor our programs to better serve your needs.

Ang seksyon kini magtudlo sa imong klasipikasyon ingon usa ka magtutudlo/trainee/estudyante. Palihug pag-check sa angay nga kahon nga magamit nimo (pananglitan, 4Ps Beneficiary, Displaced Worker, Out-of-School Youth, ug uban pa). Kung ikaw adunay kapansanan, ang mga personnel sa TESDA magtabang sa pagpuno sa mga may kalabutan nga detalye. Ang imong tubag makatabang kanamo sa paghatag og mas angay nga programa nga magserbisyo sa imong mga panginahanglan.

ADDITIONAL INFORMATION

This section includes details about your disability (if applicable), scholarship package (if you are a scholar), and a privacy disclaimer. Please review the privacy statement and indicate your agreement or disagreement. Your signature at the bottom certifies that all information provided is true and correct. Attach a recent ID picture (taken within the last 6 months) to complete your application.

Ang seksyon kini naglakip sa mga detalye bahin sa imong kapansanan (kung adunay), scholarship package (kung ikaw usa ka iskolar), ug usa ka privacy disclaimer. Palihug basaha ang privacy statement ug ipakita ang imong pagsugot o pagkontra. Ang imong pirma sa ubos magpamatood nga ang tanang impormasyon nga giprovide kay tinuod ug husto. Idugtong ang usa ka bag-ong ID picture (nga kuha sa sulod sa miaging 6 ka buwan) aron makompleto ang imong aplikasyon.

PRIVACY DISCLAIMER AND CERTIFICATION

By signing below, you agree to allow TESDA to use your contact details and other provided information for processing your scholarship application, employment opportunities, and program surveys. Please ensure all information is accurate and complete. Attach a recent ID picture (taken within the last 6 months) and provide your signature to certify that the details above are true and correct.

Pinaagi sa pagpirma sa ubos, mouyon ka nga tugutan ang TESDA sa paggamit sa imong contact details ug uban pang impormasyon nga giprovide alang sa pagproseso sa imong aplikasyon sa scholarship, mga oportunidad sa trabaho, ug mga survey sa programa. Palihug siguroa nga ang tanang impormasyon kay husto ug kompleto. Idugtong ang usa ka bag-ong ID picture (nga kuha sa sulod sa miaging 6 ka buwan) ug ihatag ang imong pirma aron mapamatud-an nga ang mga detalye sa ibabaw kay tinuod ug husto.
